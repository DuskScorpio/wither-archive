# Cobble Cage: Compact, Reactive Wither Cobble Farm

**Authors:** *Sicarious*

**Endorsed by:** *Scorpio*

**Tags:** *Tested & Functional*

**Original post:** [View on Discord](https://discord.com/channels/913065809096638494/1497618167080681504)

A Wither-based, Fully Automatic Cobblestone Farm that utilises the Cobblestone Generator itself to form the sides of the Wither Cage., with precise, reactive timing so that the Cobblestone regenerates near-instantly after the Wither breaks it. Utilises a name-tagged Creaking in a Minecart to safely absorb blue Wither Skull projectiles
## Versions
1.21.5+
## Rates
- Cobblestone: 32,200 items/hr
## Features
- Extremely compact, 8x9x7 block footprint excluding storage
- Glitchless design, no TNT-duping or bedrock breaking required. Hard-difficulty compatible
- Fully playerless, can run with a chunkloader
- Extremely reliable, survived 10,000 hours of sprinting with Carpet `moreBlueSkulls` enabled
- Extremely unload resistant
## Considerations
- Overworld only, as Creakings cannot be transported to other dimensions.
- Complex build process
- Withers are scary
- May rarely automatically shut down on unload, in which case it needs to be manually restarted. The Wither remains safely caged.
## Notes
- BUILD ENTIRELY WITHIN 1 CHUNK, as partial unloading could result in a Wither Escape
- There are 3 stages, which must be built in order, each time altering the farm until it matches the schematic, then following the instructions on the signs in the schematic.
- Stage 1 is used to place the Creaking into the farm, and push shielding blocks into place to protect the Minecart from the Wither Summoning explosion
- Stage 2 is used to summon and align the Wither within the farm
- Stage 3 is the final stage, and is what the farm should look like once fully operational.
- Power signed Sticky Piston to turn on.
- Depower signed Sticky Piston to turn off.
- If farm shuts down due to highly unlucky unload timing, turn it off and on again.
- Test environment: CPU Ryzen 5600X with Lithium, 5000 Hour Sprint
- 0.16mspt
